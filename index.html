Don't post text as a image

See why

Taking a screenshot of your code, terminal, errors or anything fundamentally text-based is counter-productive for everyone involved. The reasons for this are listed below:

  • People can't copy it

    When you managed to solve your problem, other users can't rob your code.

  • Cannot be indexed

    Images cannot be indexed in search engines. So you will not give ad money to the omp devs xd

  • Obscures important information.

    People can't see your full code, so they can't rob it.

  • Potentially more expensive.

    Remember: Some users have a shitty internet speed, so they can't see your image, so they can't help you.
